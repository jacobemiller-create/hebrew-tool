Course Curriculum

33 lessons following the Aleph Bet Packet sequence. Letters and vowels are introduced together so students can read real syllables from Lesson 1.

Intro Introduction to Hebrew Reading
— Hebrew is Read Right to Left Rule Rule of Reading #1
— Consonants vs. Vowels — Vowels Are Helpers Concept Rules #2, #3, #4
Unit 1 First Letters & Vowels Lessons 1–5
1 בּ Bet Letter "B" — blue, boo, baboon
2 ַ ָ Patach & Kamatz Vowel "Ah" — aqua, father
3 ר Reish Letter "R" — roar, rainbow
4 וּ ֻ Shuruk & Kubutz Vowel "Ooh" — rude, Jewish, blue
5 כ ך Chaf & Final Chaf Letter "Ch" — throat clearing
Unit 2 Silent Letters, Sh'va & Core Consonants Lessons 6–10
6 מְ Sh'va Vowel "ih" — big, give; or silent
7 א Aleph Letter Silent — takes vowel sound
8 ת Tav Letter "T" — Torah, tuba
9 ה Hey Letter "H" — hands, house; silent at end
10 י Yod Letter "Y" — yellow, yo
Unit 3 Completing the Vowel System Lessons 11–17
11 ֶ ֱ Segol & Chataf Segol Vowel "Eh" — bed, set
12 ל Lamed Letter "L" — ladybug, lamp
13 וֹ Cholam Vowel "Oh" — go, home
14 אֵ Tzeirei Vowel "Ay" — okay, hey
15 נ ן Nun & Final Nun Letter "N" — Nancy, none
16 מ ם Mem & Final Mem Letter "M" — mom, music
17 ע Ayin Letter Silent — like Aleph
✦   Mid-Course Review — Letters א ה ת ל + all vowels learned so far
Unit 4 Building the Full Aleph-Bet Lessons 18–29
18 פּ Pei Letter "P" — pail, purple
19 אִ Chirik Vowel "Ee" — queen, see
20 ג Gimel Letter "G" — go, gift
21 פ ף Fei & Final Fei Letter "F" — fee, phone
22 צ ץ Tzadi & Final Tzadi Letter "Tz" — pizza, nuts
23 ח Chet Letter "Ch" — throat (like Chaf)
24 שׁ Shin Letter "Sh" — shalom, shoe
25 ק Kuf Letter "K" — kite, cup
26 ד Dalet Letter "D" — door, dog
27 ו Vav Letter "V" — vine, very
28 ט Tet Letter "T" — alternate Tav
29 ב Vet Letter "V" — Bet without dagesh
Unit 5 Completing the Aleph-Bet Lessons 30–33
30 כּ Kaf Letter "K" — Chaf with dagesh
31 שׂ Sin Letter "S" — Sarah, sun
32 ס Samekh Letter "S" — same as Sin
33 ז Zayin Letter "Z" — zoo, zebra
✦   Final Review — Complete Aleph-Bet + all vowels
